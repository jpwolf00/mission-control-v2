## 1. Voice & Tone Guide

### Brand Voice Attributes

| Attribute | Definition |
|-----------|------------|
| **Authoritative** | Experts in art curation with deep knowledge |
| **Refined** | Sophisticated, elegant, premium positioning |
| **Approachable** | Warm, friendly, not pretentious |
| **Transformative** | Focus on the life-changing impact of art |

### Tone Guidelines

- **Primary Tone:** Confident but not arrogant — "we know art, and we'd love to help you discover it"
- **Emotional Appeal:** Evoke feelings of aspiration, beauty, personal expression
- **Avoid:** Technical jargon, overly academic language, pretentiousness
- **Do:** Speak to aspirations, use vivid imagery, emphasize transformation

### Brand Personality

- **Voice:** A knowledgeable friend who happens to be an art expert
- **Personality:** Warm, passionate, professional, attentive
- **Key Differentiator:** We don't just sell art — we create meaningful experiences

### Messaging Principles

1. **Lead with transformation** — Not "we select art" but "we transform spaces"
2. **Make it personal** — Every client is unique; our approach is bespoke
3. **Emphasize expertise** — Years of experience, curated network of artists
4. **Create aspiration** — Help clients envision what's possible
