## Brand Direction: "Refined Warmth"

ART Consulting speaks with confidence of expertise and warmth of creativity. Images should reflect:
- **Expert** - Knowledgeable, confident, authoritative
- **Warm** - Inviting, approachable, human
- **Curated** - Discriminating, thoughtful, selective
- **Transformative** - Inspiring, aspirational, life-enhancing

### Visual Style Guidelines
- **Color Palette:** Deep navy (#1a1a2e), warm gold (#fbbf24), soft purple (#764ba2), gradient blues (#667eea)
- **Mood:** Sophisticated yet approachable, gallery-like quality, natural light
- **Subjects:** Contemporary art, curated spaces, professional installations, welcoming environments
- **Avoid:** Generic stock photos, overly corporate sterile images, cluttered spaces
